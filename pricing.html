Frequently Asked Questions

You'll receive notifications. Tasks may be queued or throttled depending on the plan. Upgrade to increase capacity.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, anytime from your organization settings. Downgrades apply next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Free tier is forever. No credit card required.
What is Ultra?
Fully dedicated cluster with isolated Dispatch Core, private Agent Mesh, and sovereign deployment. Contact us for a quote.
Do you support on‑premise?
Yes, for Enterprise and Ultra plans.
Can I transfer Organization Ownership?
Yes. Owner can transfer rights to another user. Audit log tracks the change.
